Privacy Policy — Work & Travel Community

Last updated: 2026-04-23

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Work & Travel Community application ("App") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: Email address, display name, profile photo (if provided), unique user ID. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the email address and name you authorize. With Apple Sign In, you may choose to hide your real email — we will only receive the relayed email Apple provides.

User-generated content: Messages you send in chat rooms, friend requests, profile information you submit, reports you file.

Subscription data: Subscription status (active/inactive), purchase tokens, and transaction identifiers — processed via Apple's StoreKit and RevenueCat. We do not see or store your credit card number.

Location: If you grant location permission, the App uses your device location to display nearby jobs, accommodations, and companies on the map. Location is used only on-device for filtering and is not stored on our servers.

Device & usage data: Device model, OS version, language, app version, crash logs (via Firebase Crashlytics, anonymized).

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and authenticate your account.
  • To deliver messages, friend requests, and notifications.
  • To process auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple and RevenueCat.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs (aggregated, non-identifying analytics).

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, Realtime Database, Cloud Storage, Crashlytics. Firebase Privacy
  • Apple Sign In: For account creation/login. Apple Privacy
  • Google Sign In: For account creation/login. Google Privacy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile, messages, and friend connections are removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., subscription receipts).

6. Your Rights (GDPR / KVKK)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. Account deletion can also be initiated in-app from the Profile screen.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it.

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. Passwords are never stored — authentication is delegated to Firebase Auth.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
